Need Alcohol Server Training?

Way To Serve

Way To Serve is an exciting state-approved online training program to get alcohol server training. Servers can complete the training at their own pace, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. No need to travel to a class. Way To Serve uses a multi-media design and educational simulations to create an engaging and enjoyable training program. Way To Serve is available in California, New Mexico, and Washington state in English and Spanish. Take a look at and 'like' Way To Serve on Facebook.
